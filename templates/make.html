Lets make a quiz

Here are the rules:

1. Mark start of a question with "***" (quotation marks not included)
2. Label each question as 1) 2) ...
3. Label each option as a) b) ....
4. Mark the correct option by entering "##" at the end of that option.

So a sample would be:

***
1) How many alphabets are there ?
a) 24
b) 29
c) 26 ##
d) 30
***
2) What is the radius of Earth ?
.....
.....
